Garage Cleanout

"I hadn't parked in my garage in four years. Now I can."

Washington Township, NJ  ·  Spring 2025

My garage had reached a point where I'd stopped looking at it. Three generations of stuff — my dad's old tools, furniture we moved in "temporarily," two broken treadmills from optimistic January resolutions, and boxes I genuinely couldn't identify anymore.

I'd priced out dumpster rentals, listed things on Facebook Marketplace (sold two items in a month, gave up), and watched YouTube videos that made decluttering look fun. Lies.

A neighbor mentioned Hackettstown Junk Removal. I called Thursday, they came out Friday morning, quoted me on the spot, and came back Saturday. The whole garage took about 90 minutes for the crew. Things still in good shape went for donation. The rest went away properly. The garage was empty. I could park in it again for the first time in four years.

Sometimes the best decision is just calling someone who does this professionally.

Estate Cleanout

"We needed someone who understood what we were going through."

Hackettstown, NJ  ·  Winter 2024

After my mother passed, my siblings and I faced a 3-bedroom house in Hackettstown packed with 40 years of life. We'd been delaying it for months — it felt impossible to start, and emotionally overwhelming to think about.

We reached out to Hackettstown Junk Removal after a recommendation from our real estate agent. They came out for a walkthrough, explained exactly how they'd handle things, and were genuinely kind about the situation — not clinical or rushed.

On the day of the cleanout, they worked through the whole house in about 6 hours. Items that were still in good shape went to donation, which meant something to us. We left that day with an empty house and a real sense of closure.

I'd recommend them to anyone going through an estate situation in Warren County.

Basement Cleanout

"The basement we'd been avoiding for 7 years — gone in a day."

Oxford Township, NJ  ·  Fall 2024

We bought our house in Oxford knowing the basement was going to be a project. The previous owners had clearly used it as permanent storage for everything they didn't want to deal with. By the time we got around to it — seven years later — we'd added our own layers to the problem.

We did the sorting ourselves over a weekend — pulled out what we wanted to keep, set aside some things for Facebook Marketplace, and made a pile for the crew. Called Hackettstown Junk Removal Monday morning, they were there Wednesday.

The basement was cleared in about 3 hours. They swept up after themselves. The price was exactly what they quoted. We now have a finished basement project on the actual agenda — something we genuinely couldn't even picture before.
